<p>Note: You must also install <a href="https://f-droid.org/packages/de.k3b.android.locationMapViewer">Location Map Viewer</a> to display the results.</p>
<p>This app adds the option <em>Show Articles in Map</em> to any app that can shown/send/share a geo location or <strong>geo-uri</strong> .</p>
<p>This app hooks into the android system to intercept "view geo-uri". When getting a geo-uri it asks
encyclopedia wikipedia  (or travelguide wikivoyage or knowledgebase wikidata or photogallery wikimedia)
and shows the results in
<a href="https://f-droid.org/packages/de.k3b.android.locationMapViewer">Location Map Viewer</a>
(or in any other installed app that understands the format 'kmz').</p>
<p><em>Location Map Viewer</em> shows a map with bubbles (or article-icons if enabled/available) for every wikipedia article found.</p>
<p>If you click on a bubble you get a popup with a short article description and a button that links to the full article.</p>
<p>Here are some f-droid apps that are compatible with <em>Show Articles in Map</em></p>
<ul>
<li>navigation: <a href="https://f-droid.org/packages/net.osmand.plus/">OsmAnd</a>,</li>
<li>public transportation: <a href="https://f-droid.org/packages/de.grobox.liberario">Transportr</a> or <a href="https://f-droid.org/packages/de.schildbach.oeffi">oeffi</a></li>
<li>translate postal address to geo <a href="https://f-droid.org/packages/name.gdr.acastus_photon">Acastus Photon </a></li>
<li>Share your current position <a href="https://f-droid.org/packages/ca.cmetcalfe.locationshare">LocationShare</a> or <a href="https://f-droid.org/packages/net.mypapit.mobile.myposition">My Position</a></li>
</ul>
